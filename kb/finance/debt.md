## Debt Quality

Good debt

- grow in value
- generate long term income
- low interest rate
- counter examples
  - downside of higher education
  - risks of small business ownership
  - real estate money pit
  - volatile investing

Bad debt

- quickly loss value
- do not generate long term income
- high interest rate
- examples
  - expensive cards
  - clothes,consumables and other goods
  - credit cards

Gray

- Consolidation loans
- Borrowing to invest
- Credit card reward programs
